About StudentBuddy

Your all-in-one AI-powered academic companion designed to make student life smarter, easier and more productive.

🚀

Our Mission

StudentBuddy was built with one goal — to give every student access to powerful AI tools that were previously only available to a few. Whether you are preparing for exams, building your career, or managing your academic life, StudentBuddy is here to guide you every step of the way. We believe technology should simplify education, not complicate it.
